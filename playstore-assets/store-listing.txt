================================

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 char limit):
----------------------------------
Time your baking perfectly with step-by-step recipe timers and smart alarms.


FULL DESCRIPTION:
-----------------
SousChef is your personal baking assistant that helps you nail the timing on every recipe.

Whether you're making sourdough bread, croissants, or any multi-step baked goods, SousChef keeps track of where you are and alerts you when it's time for the next step.

FEATURES

• Step-by-step recipe timers - Never miss a fold, proof, or bake time
• Smart alarms - Get notified exactly when each step is due
• CookLang format - Write recipes in simple, readable markdown
• Photo gallery - Attach multiple photos to each recipe
• Works offline - All your recipes stored locally on your device
• Privacy-first - No accounts, no tracking, no data collection

PERFECT FOR

• Sourdough bakers tracking long fermentation schedules
• Pastry chefs juggling multiple timed steps
• Home bakers who want to nail their timing
• Anyone tired of setting multiple phone timers

HOW IT WORKS

1. Create or import a recipe with timed steps
2. Start baking and let SousChef track your progress
3. Get alarms when each step is due
4. Tap to advance through steps as you complete them

Your recipes, your timing, perfected.
